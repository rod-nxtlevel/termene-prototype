Problem 1

The signup sends the wrong signal

The "Create Account" button routes to /book-a-meeting-contact — a URL that screams "talk to sales."

The form promises: "In maximum 12 hours you'll be contacted by a consultant."

Users came to try a product, not book a sales call.
termene.ro/book-a-meeting-contact

URL: /book-a-meeting-contact
Problem 2

An AI terms gate blocks the product

Immediately after signup, users are shown an AI terms & conditions modal. They must accept before accessing any functionality.

Users didn't sign up for AI. They came to check a company.
termene.ro/dashboard
First experience after signup: a legal disclaimer
Problem 3

Wrong first experience

After navigating past the AI T&C, users land on an AI chatbot — not the company search that is the core product.

This is disorienting. Users came to search for a company, not chat with an AI assistant.
